Our Story

Founded in 2010 in the heart of Manchester, Northern Bites was born from a passion for celebrating the incredible produce of Northern England. Our chef, a Manchester native, brings over 20 years of culinary expertise to every dish.

We're proud to partner with local farmers, fishermen, and artisan producers to bring you a true taste of the North West. Our menu changes with the seasons to showcase ingredients at their peak.
